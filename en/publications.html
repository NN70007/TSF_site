Publications & sources

Academic references and external sources mentioning Teleradiology Without Borders (TWB) and its expertise network.

This page brings together seven publications documenting TWB, organised into three categories: primary (1), secondary (5) and tertiary (1). Sources are listed chronologically within each category; links point to the original publishers or repositories. Last verified: May 2026.

Primary publications

Original scientific articles and editorials describing the TWB network.

Journal de Radiologie · Vol. 89, no. 10, p. 1564
Primary source
2008·Original article

Téléradiologie sans frontières: birth of an international radiological expertise network

J.B. Niedercorn, G. Wajnapel, P. Jissendi Tchofo, M. Bali, D. Balériaux, C. Christophe.

Describes the creation of the TWB network in 2007 and the charitable WEB-RIS-PACS infrastructure (PowerServer, donated by RamSoft). Across a prospective study of 30 cases, a second opinion was judged useful in 80% of cases. Six countries were involved: Afghanistan, Benin, Burkina Faso, Cameroon, DR Congo and Rwanda.
