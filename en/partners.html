Historical partners

Historical partners

TWB’s operation relied on technology partners that contributed to the infrastructure, the connectivity tools and the operational deployment of the platform.

Special mention
Vijay Ramanathan
CEO and co-founder of RamSoft

TWB extends special thanks to Vijay Ramanathan for the technological support given to the project and for providing the PowerServer™ system that made the teleradiology infrastructure possible.

Doug Suttles
Co-founder and former CEO of Ookla

TWB also extends special thanks to Doug Suttles for enabling the deployment of connectivity-measurement tools on the project’s servers, to assess connection speeds between Africa, Europe, the United States and Latin America.

Technology partners

RamSoft

RamSoft is a medical-imaging and radiology software company. TWB relied on the PowerServer™ PACS provided by RamSoft for its RIS/PACS infrastructure, teleradiology and patient-information management.

Role
RIS/PACS infrastructure and teleradiology platform.
Contribution
Provision of PowerServer™.

Ookla

A company specialising in broadband-testing and network-diagnostic tools. Ookla’s solutions were used to qualify the network connections required for the platform and to test the links between several deployment areas.

Role
Connectivity measurement and qualification.
Contribution
Throughput tests between deployment regions.

Archive

Teleradiology Without Borders (TWB) was a Luxembourg-based nonprofit association (a.s.b.l.) active from 2007 to 2014. This website is maintained as the official historical archive.
